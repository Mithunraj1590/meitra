Success Story

A New Lease on Life: Advancing Neuro Surgery

Meitra Hospital continues to push the boundaries of medical excellence. Our recent success in complex neuro-navigation surgery has brought hope to hundreds of families.

When patient Rajesh entered Meitra, he was facing a critical spinal condition that required extreme precision. Our multidisciplinary team at the Centre of Excellence for Neuro Sciences took up the challenge using the latest O-arm surgical imaging system.

The surgery, which lasted over 6 hours, was assisted by robotic navigation, ensuring sub-millimeter accuracy. This technology minimizes risk and significantly reduces recovery time compared to traditional methods.

Today, Rajesh is not just walking; he is back to his active lifestyle, a testament to the synergy of expert hands and advanced technology at Meitra.

Technological Edge

We believe that technology in the hands of the right experts can perform miracles. Our robotic surgical systems and digital operation theatres are designed to provide the safest surgical environment in the country.
